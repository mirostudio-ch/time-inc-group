Who We Are

A Global Management Company for the Luxury Watch Industry

Time Inc. Group is a global management company, a service provider that delivers the necessary professional business infrastructure — including accounting (Fi-Bu/Be-Bu), accounts payable, accounts receivable and creditor management, human resources, sales & distribution worldwide, global marketing, communication PR, advertising, and more — for companies in and for the watch industry.

The owner has over 50 years of international experience in the luxury watch industry and has built an extensive international network of specialists in the luxury goods sector.
